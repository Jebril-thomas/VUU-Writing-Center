The Process

How It Works

Getting writing support is simple. All sessions are free and confidential for enrolled VUU students.

1

Choose Your Format

Select in-person, virtual, or asynchronous draft review — based on your preference and schedule.

2

Fill Out the Form

Complete the booking form below with your name, VUU email, session type, and preferred time.

3

Come Prepared

Bring your assignment prompt, any drafts, and a list of questions. We'll take it from there.
