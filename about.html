Our Mission

Empowering Every Writer

The Virginia Union University Writing Center is dedicated to helping all members of the VUU community become more confident, effective, and independent writers.

We believe that writing is a process, not a product — and that every writer benefits from conversation, feedback, and continued growth.

Our History

Rooted in the
VUU Tradition

Founded as part of Virginia Union University's commitment to academic excellence, the Writing Center has grown from a small tutoring program into a comprehensive academic support hub serving thousands of students each year.

VUU has stood as a beacon of higher education since 1865. Our Writing Center carries that legacy forward — ensuring every Panther has the tools and support to communicate ideas with confidence.
