Our Mission

Empowering Every Writer

The Virginia Union University Writing Center is dedicated to helping all members of the VUU community become more confident, effective, and independent writers. We provide a collaborative, supportive environment where students at every level can develop their writing skills across all disciplines and genres.

We believe that writing is a process, not a product — and that every writer, from first-year students to doctoral candidates, benefits from conversation, feedback, and continued growth.

Our History

Rooted in the
VUU Tradition

Founded as part of Virginia Union University's commitment to academic excellence, the Writing Center has grown from a small tutoring program into a comprehensive academic support hub serving thousands of students each year.

VUU has stood as a beacon of higher education since 1865. Our Writing Center carries that legacy forward — ensuring every Panther has the tools, support, and confidence to communicate their ideas with power and precision.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

01

Inclusivity

We welcome every writer — regardless of major, level, or background. All languages and writing traditions are respected.

02

Collaboration

We work with writers, not for them. Our tutors engage in dialogue that empowers students to make their own informed decisions.

03

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards of tutoring practice and academic integrity in everything we do.

04

Community

We are proud members of the VUU community and dedicated to the success of every student, faculty, and staff member.
